Help us launch

It all started from an annoyance that a group we wanted to be part of didn’t exist - a software development meetup for everyone. We’re now putting in the work to create one.

We’re looking for help to shape and realise our aim for Technically Leicester.

This group will take a broad approach to the following:

  • Software Development
  • Agile Management
  • Ethical dilemmas
  • Mindfulness and wellbeing

For our events, this is what we see so far:

  • A positive, organic, and accessible atmosphere
  • Two talks on separate topics from different speakers
  • Discussion around anything of your concern
  • The option to put forward your questions to all involved

We’re looking for anyone who can help, and also anyone who would be interested in attending or speaking at such an event.

If you’re happy to, please fill in our form with any feedback or email us on:
